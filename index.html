Welcome to Reggi's BBQ! Located in Jackson, Tennessee we pride ourselves in making the best barbecue you can find in Tennessee. Our award-winning slow-cooked, hickory pit barbecue makes the mouth water by just the thought of it. Whether its is a hankering for hot wings or a relish for ribs, we can satisfy your desires. We can promise that you will not leave hungry; for we have combined five-star taste with affordable dining. Our sweet tea, savory smoked-sausage and cheese, or sugar-coated desserts will make you beg for more. For over 30 years, we have been serving the Jackson area our covetted secret recipies. Even if you do not live near one of our locations, you can still purchase our homemade sauces to help you find the inner chef within.
