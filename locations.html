Directions

1) Travel on I-40 East from Memphis and Jackson Tennessee. (Or West From Nashville, TN)

2) Take Exit 85 South for Christmasville Rd. toward Dr Fe Wright Drive

3) Travel approximately 1 block & take a left at the first stop light you meet.

4) Take first right into Park stone Place by Pringles Park

5) Reggi's BBQ Restaurant will be at the West end of Parkstone Place on the left!

6) Enjoy the food at Reggi's BBQ!
